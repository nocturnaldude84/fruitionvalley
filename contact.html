Common Questions

Where can I buy Fruition Valley snacks?
Our Jungle Friends range is available through select retail partners. Use the contact form above and we'll point you to the nearest stockist or help with an order directly.
Are the snacks safe for young children?
Yes. All Fruition Valley snacks are 100% natural with no preservatives, no artificial colors, and no added junk. They are recommended for children above 3 years of age.
